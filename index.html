Experience the Retreat

Your Private Lakeside Sanctuary

Escape to Marrakahshe Retreat, your exclusive lakeside haven on the pristine waters of Kahshe Lake. Just under two hours from Toronto, discover 230 feet of private waterfront where luxury meets nature in perfect harmony.

Featuring eclectic high-end decor, this curated retreat offers a 50-foot walk-in sandy beach, your own dock, a relaxing sauna, and multiple fire pits for unforgettable evenings under the stars. The glamping yurt (May through September) provides a unique outdoor experience, while the home theatre ensures entertainment for the whole family.

Set against the breathtaking Canadian Shield landscape with stunning lake views, Marrakahshe Retreat is more than just a cottage‚Äîit's an experience designed for making memories.

House Rules & Cancellation Policy

House Rules

  • Check-in: 4:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM (flexible in non-peak season)
  • Maximum occupancy: 11 guests
  • No smoking inside the property
  • Pets allowed with prior approval
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 8:00 AM (respect neighbors)
  • No parties or events without prior permission
  • Guests are responsible for any damages during their stay
  • Please treat the property and amenities with care
  • Dispose of garbage properly and leave the property tidy
  • Follow all safety instructions for sauna, fire pits, and water activities
  • Life jackets must be worn on watercraft
  • Children must be supervised at all times near water

Cancellation Policy

Flexible Cancellation:

  • Full refund: Cancel at least 7 days before check-in
  • 50% refund: Cancel between 7 days and 48 hours before check-in
  • No refund: Cancel less than 48 hours before check-in

* Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after cancellation

Important Information

  • Minimum stay: 2 nights (may vary by season)
  • Cleaning fee: $150 (one-time fee)
  • Security deposit may be required for certain bookings
  • Must be 25 years or older to book
  • Valid ID required at check-in
